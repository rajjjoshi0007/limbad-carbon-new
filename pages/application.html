Water Treatment

Activated carbon is widely used in water treatment processes to remove impurities, organic compounds, chlorine, odor, and taste. It helps in improving the quality and safety of drinking water, as well as treating industrial wastewater and purifying process water in various industries.

Benefits

  • Effective and Versatile
  • Improves Taste and Odor
  • Reduces Health Risks

Applications

  • Drinking water purification
  • Municipal waste water treatment
  • Industrial waste water treatment
  • Aquarium filters
  • Swimming pool water treatment
